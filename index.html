You can contact me via twitter @Glidder_ and/or vote here for this game once the jam is over.
Thanks for playing!
CONTROLS:
- hold "a" to shoot once (reloading is automatic).
- hold "s" to throw a grenade (it can't hurt the player and it's limited to 3 for now).
- hold "up" to perform a bayonete charge. (new)
- hold "down" to crouch.
- hold "rigth" to advance.
